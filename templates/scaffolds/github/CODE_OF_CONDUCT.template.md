## Enforcement

### Reporting Incidents

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

**Primary Contact**: 
- **Email**: conduct@yourcompany.com
- **Slack**: Direct message any Trusted Committer
- **GitHub**: Report issue to any repository maintainer

**What to Include**:
- **Description** of the incident
- **People involved** (if known)
- **Date and time** of the incident
- **Screenshots or logs** (if applicable)
- **Your preferred resolution** (if any)

### Response Process

1. **Immediate Response**: Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: Review the incident thoroughly and privately
3. **Action**: Take appropriate corrective action
4. **Follow-up**: Inform reporter of resolution (if requested)

### Possible Consequences

Depending on the severity and nature of the violation, consequences may include:
- **Warning** for first-time minor infractions
- **Temporary suspension** from project participation
- **Permanent ban** from project spaces
- **Report to employer** for serious violations
- **Legal action** if required by law

## Conflict Resolution

### Disagreements and Technical Discussions

- **Focus on technical merits**, not personal opinions
- **Provide evidence** and reasoning for your position
- **Be open to alternative solutions**
- **Escalate to Trusted Committers** if needed

### Code Review Feedback

- **Be constructive** and specific in your feedback
- **Explain the "why"** behind your suggestions
- **Acknowledge good work** alongside improvement suggestions
- **Be receptive** to feedback on your own contributions
